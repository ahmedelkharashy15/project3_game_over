  Popular Games  

Assassin's Creed is a historical open-world video game series in the genre of science fiction, action adventure, and third-person stealth, consisting of thirteen main games. The game appeared on PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360, Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X, Nintendo DS, PlayStation Portable, PlayStation Vita, HP WebOS, iOS, Android, Windows Phone 7, and Wii U.

The base game in the series was developed by Ubisoft Montreal for single-player play and Ubisoft Annecy for multiplayer play, while the mobile version was developed by Gameloft and Griptonite Studios, with additional development by Ubisoft Montreal. The game was well received by users and critics, and the series is based on the novel Alamut by Vladimir Bartol. All games in the series are published by Ubisoft.

The Witcher is a fantasy action role-playing game series developed by CD Projekt Red and published by CD Projekt. It is based on the book series of the same name by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski, acting as non-canonical sequels to the story of the books.

The main series began in 2007 with the release of The Witcher, and concluded with 2015's The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. As of 2022, the series has three main standalone games, two expansion packs and seven spin-off games. The series is critically acclaimed and commercially successful, selling over 75 million units by March 2023.

eFootball, formerly known as Pro Evolution Soccer, abbreviated PES globally and Winning Eleven in Japan, is a football simulation video game series. Foot has been developed and published by Konami since 1995. It consists of eighteen main parts and several spin-off titles released on many different platforms. The series has been a consistent critical and commercial success.

In association football circles, eFootball has a long-standing rivalry with EA Sports' FIFA series. eFootball is the second largest football video game series after FIFA, and the rivalry between the two series is considered the "biggest rivalry" in the history of sports video games.
